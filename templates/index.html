Arauco Mexico

Arauco is one of the world's leading forestry and wood-panel companies, headquartered in Chile with operations in more than 80 countries. In Mexico it runs three production plants in Chihuahua, Durango, and Zitácuaro (Michoacán), manufacturing MDF boards, PB/MDP, and melamine panels. The Durango site produces urea-formaldehyde (UF) resin — the primary adhesive used across all panel products.
