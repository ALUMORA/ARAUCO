Arauco Mexico

Arauco is one of the world's leading forestry and wood-panel companies, headquartered in Chile with operations in more than 80 countries. In Mexico it runs three production plants in Chihuahua, Durango, and Zitácuaro (Michoacán), manufacturing MDF boards, particle boards (PB/MDP) and melamine panels. The Durango site also hosts a Chemical Plant producing urea-formaldehyde (UF) resin — the primary adhesive used across all panel products.

FX Risk Position
Structural position: Short USD / Long MXN
  • Purchases urea and methanol in USD on international commodity markets
  • Operates plants and pays payroll in MXN
  • Sells panels primarily in MXN in the domestic market
  • Reports and services corporate debt in USD (~USD 7.2B net debt at group level)
Arauco LOSES when the peso DEPRECIATES. Every 1-peso increase in USD/MXN raises the 6-month raw material cost by approximately MXN 18.99 million.
